    NonEquilibriumProtocol¶

    pydantic model absolv.models.NonEquilibriumProtocol[source]¶

    A model that encodes the protocol for performing a non-equilibrium switching like alchemical free energy calculation [1, 2].

    It is expected that first the electrostatics interactions will be annihilated followed by a decoupling of the sterics interactions.

    References

    [1] Ballard, Andrew J., and Christopher Jarzynski. “Replica exchange with

    nonequilibrium switches: Enhancing equilibrium sampling by increasing replica overlap.” The Journal of chemical physics 136.19 (2012): 194101.

    [2] Gapsys, Vytautas, et al. “Large scale relative protein ligand binding

    affinities using non-equilibrium alchemy.” Chemical Science 11.4 (2020): 1140-1152.
